What we are

Wander is not a booking agency. It is an invitation.

We believe travel should be a decision made with intention — not a transaction, but a transformation. The world holds infinite depths, but most of it goes unseen, passed over for the familiar and the convenient. Wander exists to change that.

We have chosen two worlds, stripped of noise, placed before you with honesty. Sylvara, where ancient rainforest exhales in the dark and time moves at the pace of roots. Azurith, where the sea holds a stillness so complete you forget the land ever existed.

Two destinations. Two states of being. One compass pointing inward.

The two worlds

Sylvara Rainforest

Borneo, Southeast Asia. An ancient canopy where the air itself seems alive. Fourteen layers of green between you and the sky. The sound of rain on a thousand leaves. A place that asks nothing of you except your presence.

Azurith Crystal Seas

Maldives, Indian Ocean. Water so clear it erases the distinction between above and below. An atoll that sits at the edge of the world. The horizon stretches without end in every direction. Come to be undone.

Our philosophy

Every plan we offer — Standard and Elite — is built around the same core belief: the most profound experiences are not found in crowded itineraries. They are found in depth. In silence. In places that feel unmanaged by time.

The Standard plans are for the traveller who wants to go somewhere real, unfiltered, immersive. The Elite plans are for those who want to surrender completely — where every detail has been considered, and nothing is left to chance except wonder.

Privacy Policy

What we store

Wander stores only what you give us — your username, your password, and the journeys you have chosen to book. Nothing else is collected, inferred, or retained.

Where it lives

All of your data is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's local storage. We do not transmit, sync, or upload your data to any server. Your information never leaves your machine.

How we use it

Your account information is used solely to identify you across sessions so you can view and cancel your bookings. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond enabling your experience on this site.

Deleting your data

You can delete all Wander data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage. Once cleared, your account and all booking history will be permanently removed.
